Yes! All our courses are completely based as part of our mission to empower domestic workers and gardeners.

Do I get a certificate?

Yes, you will receive an industry-recognized certificate upon successful completion of any course.

We provide makeup sessions and recorded materials to ensure you don't fall behind.
